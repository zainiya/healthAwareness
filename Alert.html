Hepatitis A Alert
Date: July 1, 2018
Symptoms : Fever, Fatigue, Loss of appetite, Nausea, Vomiting, Abdominal pain, Dark urine, Diarrhea, Clay-colored stools, Joint pain, Jaundice (yellowing of the skin and eyes)
Find preventive steps here: https://www.cdc.gov/hepatitis/hav/afaq.htm
